Problem 2: The Multi-City Conference Call

What Happened: Time zone problems with multiple cities can feel like coordinating passes between Messi, Neymar, and Mbappé - everyone's moving in different directions!

The Breakthrough Method - The Soccer Clock System:

Think of time zones like positions on a soccer field:

Tokyo (Right Wing) --- Berlin (Center) --- New York (Left Wing)
+7 hours ahead Base time -6 hours behind

Step-by-Step Like Building a Play:

  1. Choose Your Captain (Base Time): Always pick the middle city as your "reference point" - Berlin in this case.
  2. Map the Formation:
    • Tokyo is 7 hours ahead of Berlin
    • New York is 6 hours behind Berlin
  3. Find the Overlap - Like Finding Space on the Field:
    • Tokyo available: 8 AM - 12 PM (Tokyo time)
    • Convert to Berlin time: 8-7 = 1 AM to 12-7 = 5 AM
    • New York available: 6 PM - 10 PM previous day (New York time)
    • Convert to Berlin time: 6+6 = 12 AM to 10+6 = 4 AM
  4. The Magic Window: Where all three times overlap = 1 AM to 4 AM Berlin time (3 hours total)

Memory Device: Draw three overlapping circles like a soccer formation diagram, and shade where all three meet.
